Joan Margalef

I am a PhD Student in Economics at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona and Barcelona School of Economics. My studies are supervised by Pau Milán and Hannes Mueller.

Beyond academia, I have served as a Research Consultant for Open Philanthropy and the German Federal Foreign Office (through Fundació d'Economia Analítica).

Research interests: Public Economics, Conflict, Development Economics, Finance, Political Economy, and Applied Game Theory.
